Method

To calibrate the analysis, I used X-ray shadows. Both transverse and longitudinal positions of the source \((x_o,y_o)\) and the IP \((x_i,y_i)\) were adjusted in the calibrations. And both of them are necessary.
To evaluate the analysis accuracy, I calculated the launch angles for each shadows from positions and compared their distribution. And I also calculated the shadows of each fiducials based on a same launch angle to compare them with the real shadows.

(Scene and shadow projections)
The longitudinal position of the magnet was also adjusted. It was found that, that position would affect the angle distribution.
